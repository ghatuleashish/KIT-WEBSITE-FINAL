CAD, nicknamed the "loonie," is the currency abbreviation or currency symbol used to denote the Canadian Dollar.
CAD is the official currency of Canada and is considered to be a benchmark currency, meaning that many central banks across the globe keep Canadian dollars as a reserve currency.
